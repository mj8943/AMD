The Core of the Game

With the breakthrough “Zen 3” architecture of AMD Ryzen™ 5000 G-Series processors, you get the
computing performance that immersive gaming demands. 7nm technology is an extraordinary high-
performance and high-efficiency design, with powerful single-thread and multithread capabilities. So,
you get fast frames for a better gaming experience.

Up Your Frames, Up Your Game

In supported games, you can take advantage of features such as AMD FidelityFX™ Super Resolution. It
helps boost your framerates for high-quality, high-resolution gaming experiences on PCs running
almost any graphics card or AMD Ryzen™ processors with integrated Radeon™ graphics.1 Our state-of-
the-art upscaling algorithm transforms low-resolution input into high-resolution frames with super-
high-quality edges and distinctive pixel detail.

Performance You Deserve

AMD Ryzen™ 5000 G-Series desktop processors power the most demanding games and obliterate
multithreaded tasks like 3D or video rendering and software compiling. With up to 8 cores, 16 threads,
boost clocks of up to 4.6GHz and up to 20MB of cache, AMD Ryzen™ 5000 G-Series processors deliver
game-changing performance.

Win with the Latest Technologies

with confidence. AMD Ryzen™ 5000 G-Series desktop processors deliver incredible performance with bleeding-edge technologies, including Precision Boost 2 and Precision Boost Overdrive.4,5 Cool under pressure, these processors continue the AMD Ryzen™ tradition of exceptional 7nm architecture performance per watt.

The Ultimate Build

Configuring and customizing your rig has never been easier. AMD Ryzen™ 5000 G-Series desktop
processors are drop-in ready on AMD 500 and select 400 Series motherboards with a simple BIOS
update. They’re also ready to be tweaked and fine-tuned using AMD Ryzen™ Master. Want to jump in
the game faster? Download AMD StoreMI to get HDD storage at SSD speeds.
